About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the State Key Laboratory of Multimodal Artificial Intelligence Systems (MAIS), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), advised by Prof. Zhen Lei. My research focuses on computer vision and multimodal learning, including efficient tuning of vision-language models, open-vocabulary multi-label recognition, and generalizable AIGC/deepfake detection.

I received my B.E. degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from South China University of Technology in 2023. I am currently working toward robust and practical multimodal systems that can adapt to open-world visual recognition and trustworthy media understanding.
